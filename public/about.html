About Me

Hi there! I'm a passionate Computer Science undergrad, set to graduate in 2026, with a keen interest in blending technology and creativity. My journey in tech and design has been fueled by a fascination with web development, creative coding, and user-focused design. Recently, I've been diving into p5.js, Three.js, and Tailwind CSS to bring dynamic, visually engaging web projects to life. Whether it's designing minimal, impactful logos or developing interactive web experiences, I thrive on crafting meaningful connections between ideas and audiences. As a content manager in the insurance brokerage industry, I've honed my ability to create engaging, strategic content for platforms like LinkedIn and Instagram, aiming to boost reach and relevance. One of my latest projects involves building an online presence for SecureHapp Educare, helping students find educational opportunities abroad, and PIB Insurance Brokers, where I’ve been focused on driving engagement and brand visibility. I’m also exploring opportunities to study Computer Science at the master’s level in the U.S., as I aim to deepen my expertise in emerging fields like AI, machine learning, and network technologies—areas I had a chance to witness up close at events like Nokia’s NBUC. Events like these fuel my drive to stay ahead in tech, and I love collaborating with like-minded innovators to build future-ready solutions. Design is a huge part of my identity, too. From creating t-shirt designs for college fests to developing brand identities, I bring a unique aesthetic to my work. I enjoy experimenting with dark-mode designs, often favoring a black-and-white palette paired with Helvetica for a clean, modern look. I see myself evolving as a creative developer where tech and design intersect, and I’m continuously working to expand my skill set—whether it’s in embedded SQL, NoSQL databases, or mastering the latest tools for creating interactive, visually captivating digital experiences. When I’m not coding or designing, I’m either exploring nature on short getaways, like my recent trip to Ramnagaram and Chikmagalur, or participating in challenges like the 365 Poster Challenge to stay inspired and push my creative boundaries.
